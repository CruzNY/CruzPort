About Me

How I came about, my interests and goals.

My name is Alexis Cruz and I am currently a senior at the New York City College of Technology majoring in Computer Systems. I've been using a computer since I was 6, but in recent years I've become a power user and it has become an extension of me.I didn't start off in CS, I was originally electrical engineering until one summer in Mexico, my cousin who was entering college showed me mechatronics and I fell in love the programming aspect. A lot of the conpects such as classes, inheritence and overloading/overriding came naturally to me since my thought process worked in a similar way. After that vacation I decided to persue Computer Systems. My school's software development track revolves around Java, so that is my primary language. I like writing applications and working with databases. Away from school I like solving problems on HackerRank or LeetCode and writting automated data entry scripts with Python, but also building websites such as this one. My future goals is to one day move out to Silicon Valley and work at one of many big tech companies there. Possibly one day even work or have my very own startup. I'm still young so for the foreseable future I want to work with dedicated and passionate people that build together into soemthing great!
